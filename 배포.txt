##### A) **Lemma-Audio 경로 불일치**
```bash
# 예시: あいさつ・する (N4 동사)

- DB audioLocal: "jlpt/n4/aisatsusuru/word.mp3" (하이픈 없음)
```

##### B) **영향받는 단어들**
```sql
SELECT COUNT(*) as total_mismatch_count
FROM vocab v
LEFT JOIN dictentry d ON v.id = d.vocabId
WHERE v.source = 'jlpt_total'
    AND v.lemma LIKE '%・%'
    AND JSON_EXTRACT(d.audioLocal, '$.word') NOT LIKE CONCAT('%', REPLACE(REPLACE(v.lemma, '・', '_'), ' ', '_'), '%');
-- 결과: 5개 단어 영향
```
